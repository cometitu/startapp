How is my vote protected from modification?
Secure E2E is end-to-end verifiable, meaning that the following checks are used to detect manipulations:
  • You can check if your vote has your preferred candidate by using “Verification” before submitting the vote.
  • You can check that your vote has been correctly recorded and stored by using the “Ballot tracker” at the “Voters and Ballot tracking center”.
  • Everyone can check that the stored votes are correctly tallied via result audits published at the end of the election.
